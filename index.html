hadrian cup

Calling all stock market, investment, business, FBLA, economics clubs
and more to Shanghai's first premiere international school investment competition.

  • Who We Are

Welcome all stock market, investment, business, FBLA, economics clubs and more to the first ever Hadrian Cup!

Hadrian Cup is a competition where all teams will invest real-time in the stock market to help gain some investment experience in a fun and competitive way. The competition will be hosted by Hadrian Capital Club, a sponsored investment club based in Concordia International School Shanghai, with the ultimate goal of helping students understand the importance and relevance of finance. We will be using the MarketWatch Virtual Stock Exchange as our platform of simulation. This is a competition format in which groups of three to four students will each create one team to join the competition.

Each account will start with $1,000,000 in their investment portfolio. As a sponsorship from Hadrian Capital Hedge Fund, at the end of the competition, the top five teams with the highest returns will receive the following cash prize:

1st place: 10,000RMB + 1,000RMB*
2nd place: 5,000RMB + 1,000RMB*
3rd place: 2,500RMB + 1,000RMB*
4th place: 1,500RMB + 1,000RMB*
5th place: 1,000RMB + 1,000RMB*

 *Hadrian Capital Hedge Fund has agreed to donate an additional 5000RMB for charity causes. At the end of Hadrian Cup, the organizers will provide the top five winners with a selection of charity organizations to select from, each team will donate 1000RMB to their preferred organization.
